About Us

Green Belt Car Wash was started in the year 2009 and has ever since grown across UAE,QATAR,OMAN AND INDIA. It was a success right from the start, we were able to building a staff capacity of arround 600 employees currently.

Green Belt Car Wash has a simple concept of using idle parking time while owners leave their cars to carry out other activities such as shopping, entertainment, working, studying. etc by providing first class ecological car wash without moving the Car from its parking spot. Each unit is Operated by Just one person and the average time is 20 minutes, Our washing carts are mobile and do not leave foam, water or dirt on the floor it can be used at any indoo or outdoor parking facility.
