About Us

About Us

Greenbelt Valet Parking System, an extension of the trusted Green Belt Car Wash brand established in 2009, has expanded its premium services across the UAE, Qatar, Oman, and India. Building on our success, we have grown to a team of 600 dedicated employees.

Greenbelt Valet Parking System offers an unmatched blend of convenience and environmental consciousness. We maximize the efficiency of your time by providing seamless valet services while you focus on shopping, entertainment, work, or study. Our eco-friendly approach ensures that each vehicle is parked and retrieved with the utmost care, utilizing advanced systems that eliminate waste and reduce our carbon footprint. Whether in indoor or outdoor parking facilities, our professional valets ensure your experience is smooth, secure, and sustainable.
